Delete Your Account - Nutrition by Tactifit

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Nutrition by Tactifit app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (usually accessible from the home screen or profile menu)
  3. Scroll to the Account section
  4. Tap on Delete Account or Account Deletion
  5. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm deletion
  6. You may be asked to re-authenticate for security purposes
  7. Confirm the deletion when prompted
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User Profile: Name, email, preferences, and profile information
  • Food Logs: All food entries and nutrition tracking data
  • Water Logs: Hydration tracking history
  • Meal Plans: All saved meal plans and recipes
  • Chat History: All conversations with AI assistant
  • Shopping Lists: All saved shopping lists
  • Pantry Items: All pantry inventory data
  • Calendar Events: All calendar and event logs
  • Progress Photos: All progress photos and image data
  • Settings & Preferences: App settings and user preferences
  • Analytics Data: Usage statistics and scores
  • AI Context: All AI-generated context and insights
  • Authentication Account: Your login credentials and authentication data

Data Retention

Once your account is deleted:

  • All data is immediately and permanently deleted from our systems
  • Data is removed from both our database (Firestore) and file storage (Firebase Storage)
  • There is no retention period - deletion is immediate and permanent
  • We do not keep backups of deleted user data

Subscription Cancellation

If you have an active subscription:

  • Your subscription will be automatically cancelled when you delete your account
  • You will not be charged for future billing periods
  • No refunds will be issued for the current billing period (as per our refund policy)
  • You can also cancel your subscription separately through Google Play Store or Apple App Store without deleting your account
