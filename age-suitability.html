Age Suitability

Nutrition by Tactifit

Recommended: Ages 18+

Recommended Age

Nutrition by Tactifit is designed for users 18 years of age and older. The app requires users to be at least 18 years of age or the age of majority in their jurisdiction to create an account and use the service independently.

Age Groups & Usage

Ages 18+ (Adults)

Full access to all features including:

  • Account creation and profile management
  • AI-powered meal planning and nutrition analysis
  • Food tracking and macro analytics
  • Subscription services
  • All premium features

Ages 13-17 (Minors with Parental Supervision)

Minors may use the app under direct parental or guardian supervision and consent. Parents or guardians must:

  • Create and manage the account on behalf of the minor
  • Supervise all app usage
  • Review and approve all nutritional recommendations
  • Ensure appropriate use of health and nutrition data

Note: The app is not directed to children under 13 years of age (or 16 in the EU) per COPPA and GDPR regulations.

Children Under 13 (US) / Under 16 (EU)

The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age in the United States, or under 16 years of age in the European Union.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at privacy@tactifit.com.

Content Considerations

App Content

  • Nutritional Information: Educational content about food, nutrition, and health
  • AI-Generated Content: Meal plans and recommendations created by artificial intelligence
  • Health Data: Users may input personal health metrics, dietary restrictions, and fitness goals
  • No Inappropriate Content: The app does not contain violence, profanity, or mature themes

Why 18+ Recommended?

  • Legal Agreements: Users must accept terms of service and privacy policy
  • Health Information: App provides nutritional advice that should be used responsibly
  • Financial Transactions: Subscription services require payment methods
  • Data Privacy: Users must understand data collection and usage practices
  • Account Responsibility: Users are responsible for maintaining account security

Parental Guidance

If you are a parent or guardian considering allowing a minor to use this app:

  • Review all nutritional recommendations with your child
  • Ensure the app is used as a tool, not a replacement for professional medical advice
  • Monitor account activity and data sharing
  • Discuss healthy eating habits and nutrition education
  • Contact us with any concerns about age-appropriate content
