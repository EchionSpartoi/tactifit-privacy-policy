Privacy Policy for Nutrition by Tactifit

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, use our nutrition tracking features, or contact us for support. This includes:

  • Account information (name, email, profile data)
  • Nutrition and health data (food logs, weight, measurements, goals)
  • Device identifiers and advertising IDs
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Location information
  • Photos and audio recordings

Camera Usage

Our app uses your device's camera to:

  • Scan barcodes to identify food products
  • Take photos of meals for nutritional analysis
  • Capture progress photos for fitness tracking

Audio Recording

We use your microphone to:

  • Convert voice messages to text for AI chat
  • Record audio notes for nutrition tracking

Location Data

We collect location information to:

  • Personalize your nutrition coaching experience
  • Provide location-based recommendations and search results
  • Enhance AI assistant context for more relevant responses

Advertising and Analytics

We use tracking technologies to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns and improve our app experience. This includes:

  • Collecting advertising identifiers (IDFA) and device IDs for marketing attribution and analytics
  • Sharing data with third-party partners (such as Facebook Analytics and Firebase) to measure ad performance and understand user behavior
  • Using analytics to evaluate feature effectiveness, plan improvements, and measure audience characteristics

You can control tracking permissions through your device settings. On iOS, you will be prompted to allow or deny tracking when you first use the app. You can change this setting anytime in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

  • Firebase (Google): Analytics, authentication, cloud storage, and app performance monitoring
  • Facebook SDK: Marketing analytics and attribution (when tracking is enabled)
  • OpenAI: AI-powered nutrition coaching and chat features

These services operate under their own privacy policies. We share only the minimum data necessary to provide our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide personalized nutrition coaching and recommendations
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Measure and optimize our marketing campaigns
  • Analyze usage patterns and app performance
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes as required by law.

Your Privacy Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Tracking: Control ad tracking through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Location: Manage location permissions in iOS Settings > Nutrition by Tactifit > Location
  • Camera & Microphone: Control permissions in iOS Settings > Nutrition by Tactifit
  • Data Access: Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Data Deletion: Request account and data deletion by contacting us

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure data transmission, and regular security audits.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date below.
