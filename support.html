Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with the app?
After downloading the app, create an account using your email or Apple Sign-In. Complete your profile setup to get personalized nutrition recommendations.
How does the AI meal planning work?
Our AI analyzes your dietary preferences, health goals, and activity levels to create personalized meal plans. The system learns from your feedback and adjusts recommendations over time.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your device's App Store settings (iOS) or Google Play settings (Android).
Is my data secure?
Yes, we take data security seriously. All user data is encrypted and stored securely. Please review our Privacy Policy for detailed information about how we handle your data.
I'm experiencing a bug or issue. What should I do?
Please email us at tactifitglobal@gmail.com with a description of the issue, your device type, and app version. Screenshots are helpful if applicable.
